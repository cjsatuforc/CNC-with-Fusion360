Objectives : 
* Learn to connect to the CNC Controller at low level
* Check version, status, etc of the CNC Controller

Steps : 
1. Download Putty.
2. Connnect the CNC either through USB or Serial cable.
3. Run Putty and connect to the CNC.
4. Type a few of the GRBL-Controller commands and explain their responses.
5. Home the machine
6. Check the home position
7. Close Putty.
