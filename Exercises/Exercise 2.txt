Objectives :
Learn the Basics of G-Code

Steps : 
1. Open a text-editor - eg Notepad or notepad++
2. Write a short G-Code program to move the spindle in a 400mmx400mm square
3. Write a version which moves at rapid speed,
4. Wrote another version which powers the spindle, moves at Feed-rate speed, then powers-down the spindle
5. Test the programs by sending them to the CNC
